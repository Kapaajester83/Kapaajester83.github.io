User Notes:

VirtualBox vs VMware

In general they both work the same, VolnHub may have specific requirements
My experience is that VMware is a little more robust and stable, but also requires additional "host computer" resouces.
VirtualBox seems to be faster with smaller "host computer" resources.

Kali vs ParrotOS

Kali is similar to Linux
ParrotOS is similar to Mac

Kali Linux and VirtualBox settings

  • Debian: 64-Bit
  • RAM: 4GB
  • Video: 1GB
  • Disk: 80GB
  • Network: NAT
